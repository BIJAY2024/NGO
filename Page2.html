    What we do

    Oxfam’s vision for Nepal is a just society without poverty, in which all women and men live a life of dignity, enjoy their rights and assume their responsibilities as active citizens of Nepal. To contribute to this vision, Oxfam builds upon its niche and delivers through three inter-connected programs - a. Water Governance b. Resilience and Climate Justice and c. Gender Justice. Though these programs, Oxfam aims to provide people with opportunities to reduce poverty and inequality, ensuring that development-related activities are demand driven and sustainable gender sensitive, socially inclusive and that the most vulnerable are empowered to claim their rights. To achieve Oxfam’s goal, it works in close coordination with national and local governments and civil society organizations.
